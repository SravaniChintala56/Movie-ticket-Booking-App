Is BookMyShow trustworthy?

BookMyShow is one of India’s most trusted ticketing platforms, serving millions of users with secure transactions. We use industry-standard encryption and payment gateways to ensure your personal and financial information remains protected.

What is the refund policy for cancelled shows?

If a show is cancelled by the organiser or venue, the ticket amount will be automatically refunded to your original payment source within 5–7 business days. You will also receive a confirmation email/SMS regarding the refund.

Can I cancel my ticket after booking?

Ticket cancellation is available only for select shows that are marked as “Cancellable.” You can check this before booking. Cancellation requests must be made within the allowed time frame, and a small cancellation fee may apply.

Can I review a movie I watched?

Absolutely! Users who book and watch movies via BookMyShow can leave verified reviews and ratings. These help other users make informed decisions. You’ll find the review option under "My Bookings."
